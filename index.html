About Us

Shriraam Electronics is a trusted service provider specializing in inverters, batteries, solar power systems, and RO water purifiers. Established in 1991, we have built a strong foundation based on technical excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

With over 800+ happy customers, our services are known for prompt support, genuine products, and long-lasting performance. We aim to provide peace of mind and continuous power to homes and businesses through smart energy solutions.

Our motto, "Committed to Powering Progress", reflects our vision to move forward with innovation, trust, and consistent service - empowering every customer we serve.

Service Area: We proudly serve customers across Tiruchirapalli and are committed to assisting clients in other districts. Simply contact us, and we will connect you with our trusted engineers or partner companies for installation and setup, ensuring you receive the best service wherever you are.
